About Us

At Kiwa Energy Solutions, we are committed to delivering reliable, affordable, and sustainable energy solutions to homes and businesses across Kenya. In a world where energy demand is rapidly growing, we believe that clean, renewable energy is the key to a brighter and more sustainable future.

Our mission is to promote clean and affordable energy while helping homes and businesses reduce their carbon footprint.

Anthony Kibe is the founder and CEO of Kiwa Energy Solutions. With a strong passion for clean and sustainable energy, he has led the company to deliver innovative solar and automation solutions across the region.
